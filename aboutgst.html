About GST (Goods and Services Tax)

GST is a unified tax system introduced in India in 2017. It replaces several indirect taxes and simplifies invoicing.

How GST is Calculated?

GST Amount = (Original Cost × GST%) / 100

Example: Price ₹1000, GST 18% → GST ₹180 → Final ₹1180
