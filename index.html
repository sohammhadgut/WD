About Project

This GST Billing Website is a responsive web-based billing system developed as a course project. It helps small and medium businesses generate GST-compliant invoices, calculate taxes and store invoice history.

Project Objectives
  • Automate GST billing and reduce manual errors
  • Generate professional invoices & export PDF/CSV
  • Provide dashboard and invoice history
