About Project

This GST Billing Website is a fully responsive and interactive web-based billing system developed as a course project under Web Technology. It is designed to assist small and medium businesses in generating GST-compliantinvoices quickly with accurate tax calculations and digital record management.

Project Objectives
  • To automate GST billing and reduce manual calculation errors
  • To generate professional GST Invoices with correct CGST & SGST
  • To provide invoice storage and history for record-keeping
  • To visualize sales insights using charts and dashboard
  • To allow users to export bills in PDF & CSV formats
Technologies Used
  • HTML5, CSS3 & Bootstrap - for UI Design
  • JavaScript & jQuery - for real-time calculations and page interactions
  • Chart.js - for graphical reports and analytics
  • jsPDF - for exporting invoices to PDF format
Key Benefits
  • Simple, fast, and accurate GST billing process
  • Eliminates paperwork and allows digital invoicing
  • Enhances business efficiency and compliance with GST laws
  • Supports tracking of sales and GST amounts
  • Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

With this system, business owners can focus more on customer service and growth, while the software handles all tax calculations and record management efficiently.

Website Features

This GST Billing System provides a complete set of billing, storage, reporting and analytics functionalities designed for small and medium businesses. Below is the list of key modules included in this project:

1) Smart GST Billing
  • Automatic calculation of Amount, GST, CGST & SGST
  • Multiple items billing with HSN number support
  • Editable invoice number and date
  • Custom product support with changeable rate
  • Real-time calculation while entering values
2) Professional Invoice Management
  • Preview invoice before final print
  • Download invoice as PDF
  • Export invoice details as CSV
  • Save invoices permanently in local storage
  • Edit or delete previously saved invoices
3) Master Data (Customers & Products)
  • Add customer with GSTIN, address & contact details
  • Add products with rate for quick billing
  • Auto-fill customer details into invoice form
  • Delete any customer or product whenever needed
4) Dashboard & Analytics
  • Shows total invoices, total sales & total GST collected
  • Displays last 5 recent invoices
  • Interactive charts for business insights:
    • Sales vs GST Pie Chart
    • Monthly Sales Bar Chart
    • GST Trend Line Chart
5) Reports & History
  • Complete invoice history stored digitally
  • Quick view of invoice amount and tax breakup
  • Load any invoice back into editor for modifications
  • Export complete billing history as CSV backup
6) Modern UI & Mobile Support
  • Fully responsive Bootstrap user interface
  • SPA-based smooth navigation for faster actions
  • Easy to use on Mobile + Tablet + Desktop
7) Data Safety & Offline Support
  • All data stored safely in browser LocalStorage
  • No internet or backend/database required
  • Reset system data anytime with one click

      About GST (Goods and Services Tax)

      GST is a unified tax structure introduced by the Government of India on 1st July 2017.It replaced a wide range of indirect taxes such as VAT, Service Tax, Excise Duty, etc.GST brought the entire country under a single tax system with the slogan: ‚ÄúOne Nation, One Tax, One Market‚Äù.

      Why GST Was Introduced?
      • To remove multiple indirect taxes and simplify the tax structure
      • To reduce cascading effect of taxes (tax on tax)
      • To promote ease of doing business
      • To bring transparency and digital record keeping
      • To increase government revenue through a standard taxation system
      Types of GST in India
      • CGST: Central Goods and Services Tax ‚Äî collected by Central Government
      • SGST: State Goods and Services Tax ‚Äî collected by State Government
      • IGST: Integrated Goods and Services Tax ‚Äî applied on interstate supplies

      • Promotes digital payments & transparency
